PlaybackController

PlaybackController

Plays audio and controls speaker output on a client. The PlaybackController API provides the following event and directive messages.

Message name Message type Message description
Mute Directive Instructs your client to mute its speaker volume.
Next Directive Instructs your client to start playback of a next audio stream in a playback queue.
Pause Directive Instructs your client to pause playback of a current audio stream.
Previous Directive Instructs your client to start playback of a previous audio stream in a playback queue.
Resume Directive Instructs your client to resume playback of an audio stream.
Stop Directive Instructs your client to stop playback of an audio stream.
TurnOffRepeatMode Directive Instructs your client to turn off the single track repeat mode.
TurnOnRepeatMode Directive Instructs your client to turn on the single track repeat mode.
Unmute Directive Instructs your client to unmute its speaker volume.
VolumeDown Directive Instructs your client to turn down its speaker volume.
VolumeUp Directive Instructs your client to turn up its speaker volume.

Mute directive

Instructs your client to mute its speaker volume.

Payload field

None

Message example

{
    "directive": {
        "header": {
            "namespace": "PlaybackController",
            "name": "Mute",
            "dialogRequestId": "dialog-id-here-1",
            "messageId": "b1f88d7d-bbb8-44fa-a0a2-c5a7553e6f8a"
        },
        "payload": {}
    }
}
