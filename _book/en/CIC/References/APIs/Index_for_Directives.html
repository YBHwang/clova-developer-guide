Directive messages index

Directive messages index

Namespace Message name Description
Alerts DeleteAlert Instructs your client to delete alarms or timers.
Alerts GetAlert Instructs your client to look up alarms or timers.
Alerts SetAlert Instructs your client to set alarms or timers.
AudioPlayer Play Instructs your client to start playback of an audio stream or add it to a playback queue.
AudioPlayer StreamDeliver Returns audio stream details necessary for playback. This is a response message to an AudioPlayer.StreamRequested event message.
Clova AddMemo Instructs your client to add new memos.
Clova AddReminder Instructs your client to add new reminders.
Clova AddSchedule Instructs your client to add new schedules.
Clova CountSchedule Instructs your client to count the number of schedules within a specified period.
Clova DeleteMemo Instructs your client to delete memos.
Clova DeleteReminder Instructs your client to delete reminders.
Clova DeleteSchedule Instructs your client to delete schedules.
Clova FinishExtension Instructs your client to finish a specified extension.
Clova GetMemo Instructs your client to look up memos.
Clova GetReminder Instructs your client to look up reminders.
Clova GetSchedule Instructs your client to look up schedules.
Clova Hello Notifies your client that a downchannel connection has been established.
Clova RenderMemoList Instructs your client to display a list of memos.
Clova RenderReminderList Instructs your client to display a list of reminders.
Clova RenderTemplate Instructs your client to display templates.
Clova RenderText Instructs your client to display text.
Clova StartExtension Instructs your client to start a specified extension.
PlaybackController Mute Instructs your client to mute its speaker volume.
PlaybackController Next Instructs your client to start playback of a next audio stream in a playback queue.
PlaybackController Pause Instructs your client to pauses playback of an audio stream.
PlaybackController Previous Instructs your client to start playback of a previous audio stream in a playback queue.
PlaybackController Resume Instructs your client to resume playback of an audio stream.
PlaybackController Stop Instructs your client to stop playback of an audio stream.
PlaybackController TurnOffRepeatMode Instructs your client to turn off the single track repeat mode.
PlaybackController TurnOnRepeatMode Instructs your client to turn on the single track repeat mode.
PlaybackController Unmute Instructs your client to unmute its speaker volume.
PlaybackController VolumeDown Instructs your client to turn down its speaker volume.
PlaybackController VolumeUp Instructs your client to turn up its speaker volume.
SpeechRecognizer ExpectSpeech Instructs your client to be ready to receive speech input from a user.
SpeechRecognizer ShowRecognizedText Returns speech recognition results to your client in real time, in the form of natural language.
SpeechRecognizer StopCapture Instructs your client to stop capturing user's speech input.
SpeechSynthesizer Speak Instructs your client to play the synthesized TTS audio file through its speaker.
