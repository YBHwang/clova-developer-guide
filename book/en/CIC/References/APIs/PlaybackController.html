PlaybackController

PlaybackController

This API lets your client control audio playback and speaker output.

Note!

The PlaybackController was deprecated and is not supported any more. All the APIs which used to be provided by this namespace will be migrated to the AudioPlayer namespace in the future.

The PlaybackController API provides the following Event and Directive messages.

Message name Message type Message description
mute Directive Instructs your client to mute the speaker volume.
next Directive Instructs your client to start playback of the next audio stream in a playback queue.
pause Directive Instructs your client to pause playback of the current audio stream.
previous Directive Instructs your client to start playback the previous audio stream in a playback queue.
resume Directive Instructs your client to resume playback of the audio stream.
stop Directive Instructs your client to stop playback of the audio stream.
TurnOffRepeatMode Directive Instructs your client to turn off the repeat mode.
TurnOnRepeatMode Directive Instructs your client to turn on the repeat mode.
unmute Directive Instructs your client to unmute the speaker volume.
volumeDown Directive Instructs your client to turn down the speaker volume.
volumeUp Directive Instructs your client to turn up the speaker volume.

mute Directive

Instructs your client to mute the speaker volume.

Payload field

None

Message example

{
    "directive": {
        "header": {
            "namespace": "PlaybackController",
            "name": "mute",
            "dialogRequestId": "dialog-id-here-1",
            "messageId": "b1f88d7d-bbb8-44fa-a0a2-c5a7553e6f8a"
        },
        "payload": {}
    }
}
